Veli Yagci

Veli YAGCI (born March 14, 1989) is a
web developer, musician and
former athlete. Among his best-known works
are hospitality related works including being
Turkish Airlines Flying Chef,
Office Manager of Ocakoglu Hotel and
Supervisor of Assist Group, Inc..
He also served as a recording engineer and
400m dash sprinter. He has been over
30 countries and currently lives in
New York City since 2017
and is a freelance web developer.
